Strategic Overview

Q3 2026 — Fundraising Readiness & Operational Discipline

Blockdor is a mine finance and mining royalty company. Our immediate priority is raising capital to secure additional royalties, advance our permit portfolio, and acquire and process metal. The ERP is our internal operating system — it makes us look organised, serious, and ready to manage institutional capital.

How to use this document

Click a category tab to view your deliverables. Select yourself as Owner, set Priority and Target Date. Use ➕ Attach File on any row to upload a quotation or supporting document directly in the browser. Items marked "📋 On File" have signed documents already saved in the local project folder.

Critical Path

Brand Guideline Approval (Dawid, today/Wednesday) → Website mock-ups + SIREXE booth design (3–4 weeks) → Website live (8 weeks) → Investor Data Room populated → 2-month fundraising sprint.

Hybrid Strategy — Build, Buy, or Integrate

QuickBooks Online Advanced ($200/mo) for official books, taxes, and reconciliation. DocSend ($150/mo) for the investor data room. Custom ERP only where Blockdor has unique needs: mining royalties, permits, gold inventory, Smartbox traceability, multi-subsidiary structure.
